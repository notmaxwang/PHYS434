Part D. The number of days will be 6 days, and Y is 8. The sigma is calcualted by first accumulating the noise that is bigger than 8 * 6 which is 48, and to find the sigma the function stats.norm.ppf is used, which is 1 - the accumulated probability. The final sigma is around 3.506.

In [6]:
prob = 0
for i, val in enumerate(y6):
    if (i > 8*6):
        prob += val
        
stats.norm.ppf(1-prob)
Out[6]:
3.506376406768917
